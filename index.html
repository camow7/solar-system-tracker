Solar System Tracker

Desktop Controls

  • + / − — Speed up / slow down
  • Space — Pause / resume
  • N — Jump to today
  • L — Toggle labels on/off
  • F — Fullscreen projection mode

Mobile Controls (Touch)

  • Swipe Right — Speed up
  • Swipe Left — Slow down
  • Swipe Up/Down — Pause / resume
  • Tap — Pause / resume
  • Double-tap — Jump to today

What You're Seeing

  • Sun — Center (yellow with glow)
  • Planets — 8 colored spheres with realistic details
  • Moons — Small dots orbiting planets (Jupiter, Saturn, Neptune)
  • Asteroid Belt — Tiny dots between Mars and Jupiter
  • Spacecraft — Colored diamonds (JWST, Parker, Voyager)
  • Elliptical Orbits — Rings show planetary paths

Tips

• Time moves 1 simulated day per real second by default

• Use +/− to speed up and watch monthly changes

• Press L to toggle labels (helps identify objects)

• Press F for full-screen ceiling projection

• The Moon orbits Earth; Jupiter's moons move visibly

• All positions are real and calculated from current date
